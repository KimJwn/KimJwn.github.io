# Argentina Deepfake Law Summary

## Core Legal Framework
- Current local corpus for Argentina consists of 2025 public-administration AI directives/resolution documents, not a fully deepfake-specific criminal code package in this dataset. (Ref1, Ref2)

## Summary
- Argentina appears guideline/administrative-policy oriented in the current corpus, with emphasis on responsible AI use in public administration and governance process controls. (Ref1, Ref2)
- Deepfake-specific enforceability for election/non-consensual harms should be treated conservatively until additional official statutory corpus is added. (Ref1, Ref2)

## Policy Dimensions
- `Labeling status`: guidance-level direction is present in current documents, but a clearly extracted deepfake-specific statutory labeling mandate is not yet confirmed in this corpus. (Ref1, Ref2)
- `Election-related status`: dedicated election-deepfake codification is not clearly established in current local corpus. (Ref1, Ref2)
- `Non-consensual deepfake status`: direct codification is not yet clearly established in this corpus; further official criminal-law sources are required. (Ref1, Ref2)
- `Service-provider/platform liability status`: current corpus is centered on public-administration governance guidance rather than a dedicated platform-duty regime. (Ref1, Ref2)
- `Penalty/sanctions codification status`: sanction framework for deepfake-specific offenses remains unclear in currently loaded Argentina files. (Ref1, Ref2)

## Practice Notes (by Dimension)
- `Labeling`: apply internal disclosure controls in public-facing AI content workflows even when legal force is guidance-level.
- `Election`: treat election-context synthetic media as high-risk pending dedicated codification.
- `Non-consensual deepfakes`: use expedited intake-block-preserve workflows while statutory anchors are being expanded.
- `Service-provider/platform liability`: document notice-action-record chains to preserve auditability.
- `Penalty`: mark sanction exposure as provisional until article-level statutory sources are ingested.
